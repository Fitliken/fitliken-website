About the Band

Fitliken are a high-energy classic rock covers band based in Aberdeen, built for busy dancefloors and packed weekend venues.

Blending timeless rock anthems with modern favourites, the band deliver tight, engaging performances that keep crowds involved from first song to last. Expect big choruses, sharp stops and memorable finishes — all played with precision and purpose.

Fitliken pride themselves on reading the room. Whether it’s a lively Saturday night in a city-centre pub, a harbour-side summer event, or a private function, the focus is always on the atmosphere — bringing the right level of energy for the space.

With professional sound, an efficient setup and a growing reputation across the North-East, Fitliken offer a reliable, polished live show that works for both venues and audiences alike.
